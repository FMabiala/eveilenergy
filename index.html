Equipping South Africa’s solar, wind and bio-mass energy supply.

The increasingly high demand in electricity and the deterioration of the old grid infrastructure over the years, have led in a constant interruption in electricity supply in South Africa.

That is why we are busy building South Africa’s renewable electricity alternative, that is, from solar and wind energy supply-- not to dismiss the old, but to work synergically and in a smart way in order to reduce the reliance on coal and energy imports as well as help prevent future electricity and fuel price rises.

Éveil Energy helps you be in charge

It is more important than ever to keep control of your electricity. The use of a smart meter allows you to easily track your electricity usage and see where you can save to reduce your bills and carbon footprint.

Benefits of a smart meter

  • You are in control - you can see the cost of your electricity
  • Receive more accurate bills
  • Get a better understanding of where you use electricity in your business.
  • If you use cards, you can top up online
